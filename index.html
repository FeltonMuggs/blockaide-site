Our Mission

Heritage communities shouldn't be left behind by the technology reshaping their trades.

From the oyster grounds of the Potomac to the family farms of the Northern Neck, generations have made their living from the land and water. As provenance tracking, AI, automation and digital payments transform agriculture and fisheries, BlockAide Union makes sure these communities lead that change instead of being priced out of it.

We deliver hands-on education, certifications and real workforce pathways — built for working people, taught in plain language, tied to the trades they already know.

What We Teach

Four tracks. One smart workforce.

Each track meets people where they are and connects directly to farming, fishing and rural enterprise.

🔗

Web3 & Digital Payments

Wallets, stablecoins and direct-to-market sales so producers keep more of every dollar.

🧠

Applied AI

Practical AI for yields, weather, logistics and small-business operations — no PhD required.

🛡️

Blockchain & Provenance

Trust and traceability from the oyster bed to the buyer — proving origin and quality on-chain.

🤖

Robotics & Automation

Drones, sensors and automation that ease hard labor and keep family operations competitive.
